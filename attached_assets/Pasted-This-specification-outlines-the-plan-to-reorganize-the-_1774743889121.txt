This specification outlines the plan to reorganize the Jarvis sidebar for better approachability, promote the Lamination Studio to a primary position, and implement a dedicated Lamination Pulse card for the bakery department on the home screen.

1. Sidebar Architectural Overhaul
The sidebar will be reorganized into four high-level functional groups to reduce cognitive load while keeping critical tools at the top level.

A. Global Pulse (Always Visible)
Home: Primary dashboard.

Lamination Studio: Promoted to a stand-alone top-tier item due to its core operational importance.

Messages: Includes unread and urgent badges.

B. Production Hub (Collapsible)
Groups all "doing" activities by department to help bakers focus on their specific tasks.

Bakery Hub: Bakery (Case management), Pastry Passports, and Production Logs.

Kitchen Hub: Kitchen and Prep EQ.

Coffee Hub: Coffee Inventory and Drink Recipes.

Formula Library: Master Recipes and SOPs.

C. Operations & Support (Collapsible)
Separates operational "how-to" and scheduling from active production.

Team Ops: Schedule, Calendar, Task Manager, and Time Cards.

Back Office: Inventory master list, Vendors, and Maintenance.

Resources: Tutorials and Test Kitchen.

D. Intelligence & Data (Role-Based)
Restricted to Owner and GM roles to keep the front-line UI clean.

Business Intelligence: The Firm, Insights, The Loop, Payroll Review, and Sentiment Matrix.

Integrations: Collapsed technical hub containing Plaid, Square Settings, Square Labor, and ADP Labor.

2. Home Screen: Lamination Pulse Card
For members of the bakery department, a high-priority "Quick Access" card will be implemented on the Home dashboard (client/src/pages/Home.tsx).

Visibility: Dynamic based on the user's department attribute (where department === 'bakery').

Data Integration: Connected to the /api/lamination/active endpoint to pull real-time dough states.

Interface Elements:

Live Status: Count of doughs currently in "Turning," "Chilling," or "Proofing" states.

Rest Alerts: Visual indicator if any dough has exceeded its chilling duration and is ready for the next turn.

Action Primary: "New Batch" button to quickly initiate dough logs.

Action Secondary: Link to the full Lamination Studio for deep-dive management.

3. Implementation Steps
Refactor Sidebar Logic: Update client/src/components/Layout.tsx to use the new SidebarGroup and SidebarMenuSub components from the UI library.

Permission Filtering: Update the canSeeSidebarItem helper to ensure the Integrations hub is hidden from non-admin users.

Lamination Component: Build a new LaminationPulseCard.tsx component and register it in the Home.tsx dashboard grid.

Route Cleanup: Ensure all legacy top-level routes are properly nested under their new parent categories in the navigation logic.
