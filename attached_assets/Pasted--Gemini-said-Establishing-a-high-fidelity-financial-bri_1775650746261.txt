Correct Ratios: By pulling directly from the 5000 (COGS) and 6000 (OpEx) COA ranges, the AI will no longer flag a high "COGS" just because a rent payment (OpEx) was included in the invoice total.

Real-time Accuracy: The refresh button allows the owner to post a big US Foods invoice and immediately see how it impacts their "Burnable Cash" and margin analysis.
